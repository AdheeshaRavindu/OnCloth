How to Measure

Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape measure horizontal.

Length

Measure from the highest point of the shoulder to the bottom hem.

Sleeve

Measure from the center back of the neck, across the shoulder, and down to the wrist.

Shoulder

Measure from shoulder point to shoulder point across the back.

Fit Guide

Regular Fit: Our hoodies are designed with a regular fit. They're not too tight or too loose, providing comfortable room for movement and layering.

Between Sizes? If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more relaxed fit, or sizing down for a more fitted look.

Fabric Consideration: Our hoodies are made from cotton-polyester blends that are pre-shrunk. However, minimal shrinkage may occur after washing. Follow care instructions to maintain the best fit.

Still Unsure?

If you're still unsure about sizing, please contact us at support@oncloth.shop and we'll be happy to help you find the perfect fit.
