Shipping Information

We ship worldwide with reliable courier services. All orders are carefully packaged and shipped within 3-5 business days.

Shipping Rates

We offer flat-rate shipping to make costs clear and predictable:

Region Shipping Cost Delivery Time
Asia $10.00 USD 5-10 business days
Europe, USA, Other Regions $20.00 USD 7-15 business days

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 3-5 business days after payment confirmation. You will receive an email confirmation once your order is processed and ready to ship.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, we will send you a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to follow your package's journey to your doorstep.

  • Check your email for the tracking notification
  • Click the tracking link or copy the tracking number
  • Visit the courier's website to see real-time updates

Delivery Address

Important: Please ensure your shipping address is complete and accurate. We cannot be held responsible for packages delivered to incorrect addresses provided by the customer.

If you need to change your shipping address, please contact us immediately at support@oncloth.shop. Address changes can only be made before the order ships.

Customs and Duties

For international orders, customs fees, duties, or taxes may apply depending on your country's regulations. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in our shipping costs.

Shipping Restrictions

We ship to most countries worldwide. However, some regions may experience delays due to customs processing or local courier availability.

Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost or arrives damaged, please contact us immediately at support@oncloth.shop with your order number and tracking information. We will work with you to resolve the issue.

Questions?

If you have any questions about shipping, please don't hesitate to contact us at support@oncloth.shop. We're here to help!
