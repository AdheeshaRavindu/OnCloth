Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your hoodie, we're here to help.

30-Day Return Policy

You have 30 days from the date of delivery to return your item for a refund or exchange.

Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following conditions:

  • Item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it
  • Item must be in its original packaging
  • Tags must still be attached
  • No signs of wear, washing, or alterations
  • Proof of purchase or order confirmation required

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return or exchange:

  1. Email us at support@oncloth.shop with your order number
  2. Include the reason for your return and photos of the item if applicable
  3. We will provide you with return instructions within 1-2 business days
  4. Package the item securely with all original packaging and tags
  5. Ship the item back using a trackable shipping method

Return Shipping Costs

Customer Responsibility: Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item received was defective, damaged, or incorrect.

Defective or Wrong Items: If you received a defective, damaged, or incorrect item, we will cover the return shipping costs. Please contact us immediately with photos of the issue.

Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and process your refund within 5-7 business days.

  • Cryptocurrency refunds will be issued in USD equivalent to the original payment address
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable
  • You will receive an email confirmation once your refund is processed

Exchanges

If you need a different size or color:

  1. Contact us at support@oncloth.shop to initiate an exchange
  2. Return the original item following our return process
  3. We will send you the replacement item once we receive the return
  4. Additional shipping charges may apply for exchanges

Note: Exchanges are subject to availability. If the requested size or color is out of stock, we will issue a full refund instead.

Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged or defective:

  • Contact us immediately at support@oncloth.shop
  • Include your order number and clear photos of the damage or defect
  • We will provide a prepaid return label or arrange a replacement
  • No return shipping cost for damaged or defective items

Wrong Item Received

If you received the wrong item:

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery
  • We will arrange for the correct item to be sent at no additional cost
  • Provide a prepaid return label for the wrong item

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items returned after 30 days from delivery
  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered
  • Items without original tags or packaging
  • Items marked as final sale or clearance

Questions?

If you have any questions about returns or exchanges, please contact us at support@oncloth.shop. We typically respond within 24 hours.
